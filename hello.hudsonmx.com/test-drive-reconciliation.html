  • Utilize Baseline and Custom Reconciliation Rules

    The MX Platform includes a powerful invoice reconciliation rules engine that automatically matches invoices to the correct orders for you.

    Some rules are “baseline,” meaning they take into account standard industry practices, while others are customizable to meet your individual needs.

    • Auto-match based on standard industry practice

  • Setup Custom Rules

    These rules can be set up at any level of granularity—at the company, vendor, media type, client, product, or estimate level—and can run either “on-demand” with the click of a button or set to run at the same time every day.

    • Set up custom rules - at any level of granularity

  • Quickly Uncover the Details of Discrepancies

    In the matching details screen, invoices can be matched to orders by bottom line or line-by-line, depending on the media type. Users can view both side by side on a single screen and review the details to see what is matched vs. unmatched and the reasons for the discrepancy.

    All the data and details are connected, so whether you’re a buyer or on the finance team, you can quickly navigate to the relevant order, view the start and end dates, filter through media types, and more – all without leaving the MX platform.

    • View invoices and orders side by side on a single screen
    • Easily see reasons for a discrepancy

  • Complete Discrepancy Resolution Analysis

    Discrepancies can be easily found and rectified by comparing the invoiced versus ordered line items (for both media lines and non-media lines) within a single screen.

    • Solve many issues in a few clicks. Relevant data is all at your fingertips
